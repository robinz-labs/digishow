Dynamic Media Presentations on Screen
=====================================

You can display images or play videos through Screen interface in DigiShow, and dynamically adjust the property parameters of these media clips — such as scaling, opacity, and position offset — during the presentation to create animations.

In this example, the beat signals generated by the Beat Generator drive these property changes, creating animated effects on the screen.

Additionally, by setting the background image to semi-transparent and adjusting the color channel properties of the screen's Backlight, you can achieve even richer color rendering effects.
