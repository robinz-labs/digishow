Audio Player
============

This is a set of interesting examples of using the Audio Player interface.

1_audio_player
Launch playback of specific audio clips and dynamically mix them. To play multiple audio clips simultaneously, uncheck "Play Alone" in the audio clip's Options settings.

2_audio_cues
Place the actions for starting audio playback and adjusting volume onto a specific CUE timeline. Right-click the preset launch button and select "Edit Preset" to view and modify these CUE timelines.

3_volume_presets
Adds presets for adjusting the playback volume of the audio.
