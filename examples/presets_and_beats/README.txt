Presets & Beats
===============

Preset Launcher and Beat Maker are key components in DigiShow. These lighting console examples demonstrate their functions:

1_rgb_fading.dgs
Move the Red-Green-Blue faders to manually adjust light colors.

2_color_presets.dgs
Save Red-Green-Blue channel values as color presets, then launch them from the Preset Launcher panel.

3_motion.dgs
Create multiple signal links from the Beat Maker to the Preset Launcher, triggering color presets on different beats to create dynamic lighting effects.

4_motion_presets.dgs
Enable/disable beat-to-preset links to produce different dynamic lighting effects. These LINK states can also be saved as presets, allowing quick switching of lighting motions from the Preset Launcher panel.
